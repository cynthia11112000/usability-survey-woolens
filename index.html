Background Questions

Pre-task context

Prior familiarity with Woo dossiers or government information requests
How comfortable are you with reading official documents or government communication?

Task 1

First Impression

Take a moment to look at the tool. Without clicking anything yet, tell me what you think this tool does.

Task 2

Finding Internal Communications

A source tells you that Minister Grapperhaus and Prime Minister Rutte were in contact on 19 November 2021 about events in Rotterdam. Find out what they discussed.

Outcome

Task 3

Email Correspondence

You are researching how the Dutch government internally discussed the AVVN resolutions on the conflict between Israel and the Palestinian Territories. Find any email correspondence related to this and tell me what you can learn from it.

Did they understand sender/receiver info?
Did they notice redactions?

Task 4

Timeline

Open the timeline view of this dossier. Tell me what you see and what you can learn from it. Can you find when the discussion about the Dutch position started and how it developed over time?

Did they use day summaries?

Task 5

Document Exploration

The dossier also contains the actual position document on the AVVN resolutions. Find it and tell me what kind of document it is and what it contains.

Did they understand the document type label?
Did they use the original source link?

Task 6

Redaction Analysis

Look across this dossier and tell me: what information has been withheld, and under which legal grounds? Does anything strike you as unusual?

Did they find redaction statistics?
