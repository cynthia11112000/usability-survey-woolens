Session Flow

  1. Record session & condition details.
  2. Capture demographics and Woo knowledge score.
  3. Guide the participant through tasks 1–6.
  4. Complete the SUS ratings.
  5. Fill in Quick Guide evaluation (Condition C only).
  6. Capture closing reflections and submit.
